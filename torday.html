Torday Institution Explorer

This app turns the formal institution into an interactive causal model. Each slider changes one level of the structure: cosmic conditions, symbiogenic resilience, cellular homeostasis, epigenetic accessibility, lung signalling, and disease stress. A Monte Carlo engine then samples thousands of possible cell trajectories and estimates the probability of healthy alveolar homeostasis versus cellular regression / fibrosis.

What the model is doing

The app treats the institution as a linked causal chain. The stellar layer sets the quality of the biochemical platform. The symbiogenic layer sets how coherently the cell integrates inherited organelle functions. The cellular and epigenetic layers determine whether the cell can hold a modern adaptive state or whether older fallback programs are easily re-opened. The lung signalling layer implements the Torday mechanism: stretch supports PTHrP, PTHrP supports lipofibroblasts, lipofibroblasts support leptin and surfactant, and that supports alveolar health. The disease layer introduces hyperoxia, inflammation, and microgravity-like mechanosensory suppression. Monte Carlo simulation samples noisy versions of these conditions to estimate how often the system lands in health, stress compensation, reversible regression, or fibrosis.

How the calculation works

For each run, the simulator perturbs your slider settings with random noise. It then computes a chain of latent variables:

Cosmic support Biochemical support Symbiogenic coherence Cellular homeostatic capacity Epigenetic fallback pressure PTHrP effective strength Lipofibroblast support Fibrotic drive Net viability

Health probability rises when stretch, PTHrP, PPARγ, mitochondrial resilience, and selection flexibility outweigh inflammatory, oxidative, and fibrotic access pressures.

A run is classified as healthy when net viability remains high and fibrotic drive stays low. It is classified as reversible regression when the system falls into an older fallback state but retains enough reserve and flexibility to recover. It is classified as disease / fibrosis when fibrotic drive is high, PTHrP signalling is suppressed, and reserve collapses.

This is not a medical diagnostic model. It is an explanatory simulation built from the institution structure above so you can inspect how the levels constrain each other.
